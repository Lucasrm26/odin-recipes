Brigadeiro

Photo by: Meggy Kadam Aryanto from pexels

A very popular candy in Brazil, often sold on the streets in its “gourmet” version.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter or margarine
  • 3 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • Chocolate sprinkles

Instructions

  1. Add the sweetened condensed milk, butter, and cocoa powder to a deep saucepan.
  2. Stir over medium heat until the mixture pulls away from the bottom of the pan.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a plate and let it cool completely.
  4. Once cooled, grease your hands with butter and roll the brigadeiros into the desired size.
  5. Roll the brigadeiros in chocolate sprinkles.
