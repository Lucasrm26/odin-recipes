Brazilian rice fritters

Photo by: Vitaliy Haiduk from pexels

A common starter in Brazilian restaurants and bars.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of cooked rice
  • 3 beaten eggs
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Place all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Mix until a firmer dough forms.
  3. Shape the fritters into the desired form.
  4. Fry them in hot oil until golden brown.
  5. Let the excess oil drain on paper towels and serve.
