Breaded chicken

Photo by: SAAD EMRIS from pexels

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast fillet
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs
  • Salt, black pepper, and garlic to taste

Instructions

  1. Season the fillet and let it rest for 5 minutes.
  2. Pour the beaten eggs and the breadcrumbs into separate bowls.
  3. Dip the fillet in the eggs, then coat it with the breadcrumbs.
  4. Fry and serve.
